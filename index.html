    League Leaderboard is a simple stats table android app for League of Legends. Users can see where they stand compared to their friends in a variety of stats and queue types. http://evanhk.github.io/League_Leaderboard/riot.txt

    Features

    • Sort-able by Wins/Kills/Assists/CS/Neutral Monsters/Turrests
    • Supports unranked and ranked solo/duo queue
